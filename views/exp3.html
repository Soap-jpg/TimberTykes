Experiment Explanation

MAKING A SIMPLE WOODEN BOX

Objective

Learn fundamental woodworking skills by creating a simple wooden box, mastering basic joinery techniques and assembly methods!

Materials Needed

  • Pre-cut wooden boards (4 sides, 1 base)
  • Wood glue (Fevicol Marine)
  • Small nails or screws
  • Hammer or screwdriver
  • Sandpaper (medium and fine grit)
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil
  • Safety goggles

Steps

  1. Measure and mark joining points on all pieces
  2. Apply wood glue to the joining edges
  3. Assemble the sides first, securing with nails/screws
  4. Attach the base to complete the box structure
  5. Wipe away excess glue with a damp cloth
  6. Let the glue dry completely (follow product instructions)
  7. Sand all surfaces and edges for smooth finish

Skills Developed

  • Basic measuring and marking
  • Wood joining techniques
  • Assembly and construction skills
  • Proper tool handling
  • Glue application and cleanup
  • Structural understanding
  • Workshop safety practices
