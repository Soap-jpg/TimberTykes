Experiment Explanation

CREATING CROSS JOINT

Objective

Practice making strong, interlocking joints

Materials Needed

  • Small wood pieces
  • Chisel
  • Hacksaw
  • Try-square
  • Fevicol

Steps

  1. Take the given wooden block and mark according to the given diagrams
  2. Cut and chisel out the shapes
  3. Sand all the parts using the sandpapers and smooth the edges
  4. Fit the pieces together, apply fevicol and adjust for snugness

Skills Developed

  • Precision cutting
  • Chisel control and accuracy
  • Patience and attention to detail
  • Problem-solving for joint fitting
  • Traditional woodworking methods
