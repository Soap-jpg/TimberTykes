Experiment Explanation

SANDING FOR A SMOOTH FINISH

Objective

Learn how to transform rough wood into a smooth surface by mastering different sanding techniques and understanding wood grain patterns!

Materials Needed

  • Sandpaper (coarse, medium, fine)
  • Wood piece to sand
  • Safety goggles
  • Dust mask
  • Sandpaper holder (optional)

Steps

  1. Examine and feel the wood's initial rough texture
  2. Begin with coarse-grit sandpaper (80-100 grit)
  3. Sand along the wood grain in long, even strokes
  4. Switch to medium-grit (120-150) and repeat
  5. Finish with fine-grit sandpaper (180-220)
  6. Compare the before and after textures

Skills Developed

  • Surface preparation techniques
  • Understanding wood grain patterns
  • Proper sandpaper grit selection
  • Consistent sanding motion
  • Texture evaluation skills
  • Workshop safety awareness
