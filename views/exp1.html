Experiment Explanation

STRAIGHT CUTS ON A WOODEN BOARD

Objective

Learn to measure, mark and make precise straight cuts on wooden boards.

Materials Needed

  • Wooden board
  • Measuring tape
  • Penicl
  • Handsaw
  • Try-Square

Steps

  1. Measure and mark lines at regular intervals on the board using the measuring tape and pencil
  2. Secure the board firmly to your work surface
  3. Practice sawing along the marked lines, keeping the saw at the correct angle
  4. Check the cuts for accuracy using a square
  5. Adjust your technique as needed for straighter cuts

Skills Developed

  • Precise measuring and marking
  • Straight sawing technique
  • Quality control using a square
  • Hand-eye coordination
