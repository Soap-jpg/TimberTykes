Experiment Explanation

BUILDING A SMALL SHELF

Objective

Learn practical shelf construction techniques including measuring, assembly, and wall mounting to create a functional storage solution!

Design Tip: Sketch your shelf design first, considering what items it will hold and where it will be placed.

Materials Needed

  • Wooden planks (shelf board and supports)
  • Wood screws (assorted sizes)
  • L-brackets or shelf brackets
  • Wall anchors (if needed)
  • Drill with appropriate bits
  • Screwdriver
  • Sandpaper (medium and fine grit)
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil
  • Level
  • Safety goggles

Steps

  1. Measure and mark your shelf dimensions on the wood
  2. Cut the main shelf board and support pieces to size
  3. Sand all pieces smooth, especially edges
  4. Assemble the shelf frame (supports + shelf board)
  5. Pre-drill holes and secure with screws
  6. Mark bracket positions on the wall using a level
  7. Install wall anchors if needed
  8. Mount the shelf securely to the brackets
  9. Test for stability and make adjustments

Skills Developed

  • Practical measuring and planning
  • Structural design principles
  • Precision drilling techniques
  • Wall mounting and hardware installation
  • Weight distribution understanding
  • Project planning and execution
  • Problem-solving for home projects
Challenge Option: Try designing a corner shelf or adding decorative elements like routed edges.
